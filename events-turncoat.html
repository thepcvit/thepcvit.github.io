Turncoat

You are your own opponent

Turncoat is an event orgainzed by THEPC under GraVITas '19. Turncoat, popularly known as Block and Tackle or The Devil's Advocate, is a form of debate where the speaker literally debates against themself. The very reason for the name is because the player changes their stance midway through the speech to oppose their original stance. The participant will be judged on the basis of:
- Validity of the arguments
- Fluency while shifting sides
- Their grammar



Venue: TT-312
Date: 11 October 2019
Time: 2PM - 5PM
Price: ₹100
