About The Team

To say that we are simply a group of like-minded individuals would be a grave understatement. We are a family with a place for every interest, every thought and every individual. Corny, but true. Writing, debating, speaking - we do it all!

Like every well-oiled machine, we have several critical parts responsible for our own functionalities; each with their own specialization.

Media Team: They can be found covering every major happening on campus. Comprised of a bunch of really hard working folks, they're responsible for crafting strong relationships with other clubs and chapters. They are in a way - the very heart and soul of THEPC.

Design: A bunch of creative folks with impeccable visual taste. Every graphical post and wacky poster can be credited to them.

Editorial: Home to some really talented writers and thinkers, the editorial team is responsible for coming up with beautiful write-ups and thought provoking blog-posts. Make sure you aren't rusty with your grammar when speaking with an editorial member nearby.

R & D: The think-tank of the club. They're the brain behind every event; truly a force to reckon with! Think twice before diving into a rapid-fire round with them.

Operations: The very backbone of the club. As the name entails they ensure the basics responsible for the functioning of the club. They are responsible for the venue permissions, event approvals and a lot more.

Outreach: A relatively young branch, it's constituted of ongoing, enthusiastic individuals with an affinity for fests. They've been responsible for garnering many laurels for the club and will continue to do so in the future.
