Liberté

Greeno | 26 July '19 | 6PM

Liberté is an on stage event organized by THEPC as our first event for the Fall Semester 2019. Participants can simply do an on-spot registration and share their literary work. The stage is open for all from 6PM onwards. There are other games and acitives for those who are not so stage-friendly. Everyone is welcome to watch, play, or perform for FREE of cost. Still not satisfied? Come and advertise your Instagram account, personal blog, YouTube channel or anything else that you feel others should know about on the Greeno Screen
