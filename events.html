Career Convention
Previous Next

The Hindu Education Plus Club of VIT University organized an event comprising of seminars by education stalwarts titled 'The Career Convention'. The Career Convention was an event where professionals from various educational institutionals came together and helped the students broaden their horizons on the various prospects of higher education available to them after graduation.

The topics covered were GATE, CAT, GRE, GMAT, NMAT, UPSC for post-graduation. The topics relevant to the undergrads were internships and publishing of Research Papers.

ISB-YLP Sessions
Previous Next

The ISB Info-session was a seminar for the students on how to prepare for admissions to the Indian School of Business. The session was conducted by resource people from the Admissions division of the Indian School of Business. The information session was truly an eye opener which helped the students break down the complexities of the admission procedure to the prestigious institute.

A lot of good reviews were received and the response was truly ephemeral. Flawless implementation made this one of our biggest events yet and TT Gallery was a marvelous spectacle to behold, full of proliferating students looking for a path to achieve their ambition and aspirations. The Hindu Education Plus Club managed to carry out its vision of promoting education and has now become synonymous with the idea and notion of a club that has truly stood apart from others.

Odyssey
Previous Next

Odyssey is a stepping stone in the club's social endeavour to promote English speaking and writing skills amongst the youth. The main aim of the event was to provide an effective platform to the schools students for spreading their revolutionary ideas through an array of interesting events. The whole event was divided into five competitions: Inquizitions, Unveil the Meaning, Word Brawl, Spin a Yarn, and Impressario; which mainly focused on the student's general awareness, creativity, their public speaking abilities and their ability to write on varied topics.
