Career Convention
Previous Next

The Hindu Education Plus Club of VIT University organized an event comprising of seminars by education stalwarts titled 'The Career Convention'. The Career Convention was an event where professionals from various educational institutionals came together and helped the students broaden their horizons on the various prospects of higher education available to them after graduation.

The topics covered were GATE, CAT, GRE, GMAT, NMAT, UPSC for post-graduation. The topics relevant to the undergrads were internships and publishing of Research Papers.

ISB-YLP Sessions
Previous Next

The ISB Info-session was a seminar for the students on how to prepare for admissions to the Indian School of Business. The session was conducted by resource people from the Admissions division of the Indian School of Business. The information session was truly an eye opener which helped the students break down the complexities of the admission procedure to the prestigious institute.

A lot of good reviews were received and the response was truly ephemeral. Flawless implementation made this one of our biggest events yet and TT Gallery was a marvelous spectacle to behold, full of proliferating students looking for a path to achieve their ambition and aspirations. The Hindu Education Plus Club managed to carry out its vision of promoting education and has now become synonymous with the idea and notion of a club that has truly stood apart from others.

Odyssey
Previous Next

Odyssey is a stepping stone in the club's social endeavour to promote English speaking and writing skills amongst the youth. The main aim of the event was to provide an effective platform to the schools students for spreading their revolutionary ideas through an array of interesting events. The whole event was divided into five competitions: Inquizitions, Unveil the Meaning, Word Brawl, Spin a Yarn, and Impressario; which mainly focused on the student's general awareness, creativity, their public speaking abilities and their ability to write on varied topics.

Logo Quiz
Previous Next

The Logo Quiz is an event where the participants team up in pairs and guess the name of the companies from the logos revealed to them. Each logo has a short description of the organization written below to aid the contestants. The quiz has 5 levels, each level more challenging than the other as the game progresses. The event usually starts off with a short introduction as to what the event is about. The quiz is filled with knowledgeable nuggets about the companies whose logos are displayed. From fast food joints to automobiles, the game covers it all.

Renaissance Sessions
Previous Next

In this competitive and modern world of ours, to survive, we need to constantly update and adapt ourselves to the situation and the means of this change is through language. When language is so important in one's life, it makes obvious sense that literary skills, the handle of the blade, needs to be sharp and firm. Renaissance, THEPC's biweekly activity, is a bold step in sharpening the blade. Renaissance gives a "re-birth" to one's literary skills and nurtures the same. It involves reading, speaking, debating and writing thereby acheiving overall personality development of its members. Weekly sessions help in the quotient improvement about the latest happenings around the world and discussion about the same leading to culmination of thoughts. It also helps sharpen the member's speaking abilities and makes them ready to take a deep plunge into the world of corporate affairs.

Tamil 101
Previous Next

Tamil 101 was a phenomenally amazing event. The turnout was high and everyone was geared up and in full spirit. The event was specifically targeted towards the young and enthusiastic crowd of fresh minds who would have otherwise had a tough time in getting used to the local language.

The basics of the language were explained by our very own club members who are proficient and fluent in it. From your day-to-day jargon, to numbers and to those essential words such as water, food, brother, and sister, we made sure that the participants would never face any problem hereafter in finding their way in and around VIT.

Magnas

Magnas was a business themed event hosted by our club during Gravitas 2015, which brought all the biz-quizzers, marketing geeks and ad experts of our university out. A team event, it pushed the participants to the extreme with nerve-wrecking questions from the business world in the first round. The scores were carried over to the next round, where the marketing skills of the participants were checked. They were given famous brands and were asked to give an idea for a product which wasn't in the given brand's domain. On top of that, they had to convince the panel of judges the reason why their product would be a success in today's market.

The event brought out about 20 people and all of them enjoyed being a part of it. It enriched the participants knowledge about business trivia and helped them understand the basics of marketing.

NMAT/GMAT By GMAC

The Hindu Education Plus Club (VIT Vellore) would be organising a session on NMAT by GMAC on 13th December 2017. NMAT by GMAC is an MBA entrance exam, conducted by Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC), which opens doors to the leading management programmes in India. The sole motive of the session is to help the MBA aspirants have a clear vision about this examination.

All those writing the exam this year, this session would add a cherry to the cake of your preparations. While for all the future examinees, this would be the best step towards your goals
